BTS: Max Issues / All browser related Issues

For more information of how to clear history, cookies, and cache please check in the BTS Word Document

  1. Clear your browser history
  2. Go to chrome settings > advanced > scroll to the bottom and restore to default settings
  3. Close chrome
  4. Open command prompt > Start > Command Prompt
  5. Inside the command window type the following command: ipconfig /flushdns
  6. Relaunch Chrome and max

BTS: VPN Issues

  1. Check to see you are Hardwired into your router with an ethernet cable if possible and on a 2.4 Ghz connection
  2. Open task Manager and End task for ‘Cisco AnyConnect Mobile Client’, reopen and retry
  3. Attempt to switch VPN connections i.e., if on US Two Factor attempt the WEC Two Factor
  4. If VPN still will not connect attempt resetting your IP address by following these steps:
    1. Close out VPN completely.
    2. Press the start button and type in CMD
    3. Open Command Prompt
    4. Copy and Paste in Command Prompt: ipconfig /release
    5. Wait a minute until its done
    6. Copy and Paste in Command Prompt: ipconfig /renew
    7. Wait a minute until its done
    8. Restart VPN
  5. If you have VPN that drops multiple times, please run a speedtest to ensure that your internet is meeting the requirements per the Work from Home agreement.
    • Make sure you are off of the VPN and then go to speedtest.net

BTS: Oracle Issues

  1. Click the yellow and blue folder icon on your start bar
    • Alternately you can open your recycle bin and complete the remaining steps
  2. Go to “THIS PC”
  3. Go to “C Drive”
  4. Go to “Users”
  5. Go to your user id
  6. On the top of the windows explorer box (the file folder you opened) and click view and make sure the box for hidden items is checked
  7. Go to “App Data”
  8. Go to “roaming”
  9. Right click and delete the right now folder.
  10. Relaunch oracle

Clearing Oracle Cache

  1. Click on 'File' in the top left corner.
  2. Select 'Options' and go into your personal settings.
  3. Then look at the 'Local Data' section and select rebuild local data cache, then click ok to finalize.

BTS: Clearing a Duplicate Email in Oracle

  1. Open Ref#170212-004690
  2. Inside this old CR ticket click the search glass and look for the drivers email
  3. Update the contact with the driver.
  4. Right Click on the drivers name and open the profile. Then delete the email address and click the green check at the top.
  5. Save the ticket.
  6. Go to your new ticket and clear the email address. Click in another field and then back into the email box and put the email in.

This should clear the duplicate email in oracle.

BTS: Clearing a Ticket Stuck in Inbox

  1. First you will need to change the disposition of the ticket to either 'No Value' or 'Move to other Queue'
  2. Save ticket
  3. Change dispostion back to the original value
  4. Save ticket again and refresh inbox

This should clear stuck ticket from inbox, also ensure you have your ticket in the correct status of 'Closed' or 'Unresolved'

Sound Troubleshooting

  1. open sound settings to verify computer is registering your headset and mic.
  2. if it is only a Maxx/VCC error please troubleshoot VCC.
  3. After attempting these and still having sound issues please reach out to a TL for further assistance.

BTS: System shutdown abort

  1. Open command prompt
  2. Type in the following command without the quotation marks: “shutdown -a”
  3. If this does not close the shutdown window please move forward with the restart.
